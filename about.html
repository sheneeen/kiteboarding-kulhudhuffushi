About Kiteboarding

Kite Boarding is an extreme water sport where the kiters use the wind power to be pulled on water surface. This is a very demanding and loved sport in Kulhudhuffushi City. It can not only be done as a hobby as its so fun and interesting, but also it keeps oneself physicaly fit, improving muscle strength and providing energy boost.

Many kite Boarding events have successfully been conducted by the dedication and effort put by the enthusiastic kiters and organizers. Kite light events have so far been the most admired events hence they fascinate the audience as its a "Firework in the sea".

Interested kiters are always welcome to join the Kite Boarding community of Kulhudhuffushi City
